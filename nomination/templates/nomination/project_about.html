Firefox

Google Chrome

Internet Explorer

If your Links Toolbar is not visible, right click on the menu bar and select "Links." Right click on this link {{project.project_slug}} Nominator, and select "Add to Favorites..." For the "Create in" field, select "Links" then click "Add."
